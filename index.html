About Us

AlumPack Food Packaging LLC is a leading provider of high-quality food packaging solutions in the UAE. We specialize in manufacturing and supplying a wide range of aluminium containers, foils, and cling film that are perfect for food packaging. Our products are made using only the highest quality materials, ensuring that they are safe, durable, and hygienic.

At AlumPack, we believe that the packaging is an essential part of any food business. That's why we're dedicated to providing our customers with innovative and reliable packaging solutions that meet their unique needs. We take pride in our commitment to quality, service, and sustainability.

Our Products

Aluminium Containers

We offer a wide range of aluminium containers in various sizes and shapes. Our containers are perfect for packing food items such as biryanis, curries, noodles, and more. They are oven-safe, freezer-safe, and can be used for both hot and cold food items.

Foil Rolls

We supply high-quality aluminium foil rolls that are perfect for food wrapping and storing. Our foil rolls are strong, durable, and can withstand high temperatures, making them perfect for use in ovens and grills.

Cling Film

Our cling film is perfect for wrapping food items such as sandwiches, fruits, and vegetables. It's easy to use, and its strong cling ensures that the food stays fresh for longer.
